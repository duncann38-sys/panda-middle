Inside the app

Every screen, explained

Built for the generation that lives in apps — clean, fast, completely intuitive.

Venue Discovery
Step 1

Find your venue

Tap any Panda venue to see photos, ratings, the full menu and opening hours. Every venue listed accepts Panda credit.

  • Live venue discovery via Google Maps
  • Star ratings and Google review counts
  • Reserve a table directly from the venue page
  • Browse the full menu before you arrive
Browse & Order
Step 2

Browse the full menu

Scroll through the live menu by All day, Brunch, Lunch or Dinner. Deal prices in yellow are exclusive to Panda users.

  • Real-time menus powered by Deliverect
  • Dietary filters: Vegan, Gluten free, Halal & more
  • Panda-exclusive deal prices shown in yellow
  • One-tap add to cart, pay with virtual card
Smart Filters
Step 3

Personalise your experience

Filter by dietary requirements — Gluten free, Vegan, Halal, Kosher, Organic, Paleo and more. Panda saves your preferences automatically.

  • 9 dietary options including Lactose free & Spicy
  • Filters apply instantly across the full menu
  • Allergen warnings clearly flagged per dish
  • Preferences saved to your profile permanently
Dish Detail
Step 4

Every dish in full detail

Tap any dish for ingredients, dietary tags and allergen warnings before ordering. No surprises when the bill arrives.

  • Full ingredient descriptions per dish
  • Allergen warnings highlighted before ordering
  • Tags: Vegetarian, Vegan, Sugar free, Nut free
  • One-tap add to cart — pay with Panda credit
